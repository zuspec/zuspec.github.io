Zuspec

Zuspec, a portmanteau of zusammen (German for “together”) and specification, is a suite of tools for working with action-relation-level (ARL) modeling descriptions. These descriptions enable co-specification of design and test functionality, as well as co-specification of hardware and software aspects of a design. Of course, Zuspec also enables use of ARL modeling descriptions targeting a single domain – for example, embedded software tests for SoC-level verification.

Zuspec is currently in its infancy, with most effort going toward infrastructure development. Watch this space for more details.
